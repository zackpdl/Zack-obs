## Daily Workflow
1. Open [[Templates/Daily Fitness Log]] and create today's log in `Calendar/Logs/YYYY-MM-DD Fitness Log.md`.
2. Enter meals with the `macros` plugin block.
3. Record workout, run, sleep, steps, and notes.
4. At night, sync frontmatter totals so your graphs stay accurate.

## Folder Map
- `020 Health/Fitness/`: daily logs
- `020 Health/*.md`: dashboards, plans, and reviews
- `Nutrition/`: food database used by the macros plugin

## Priority Rules
- Sleep before cardio.
- Recovery before intensity.
- Consistency before perfect programming.
- Do not force 5 AM runs on 4 to 5 hours of sleep.
