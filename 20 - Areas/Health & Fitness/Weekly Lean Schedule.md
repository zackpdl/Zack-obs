## 🛡️ The Backup System

> *"If you can't do it at the time it's planned, you don't skip it — you move it."*

| Missed | Move To |
|--------|---------|
| 🧘 Morning Meditation | Lunch break, or before bed |
| 🗣 Thai practice | Any 5-min gap in your day |
| 📖 Reading | Before sleep (replace phone scroll) |
| ⌨️ Typing | While waiting / commuting |
| 🏋️ Gym session | Sunday (compress 4th session) |
| 🏃 Run | Sunday long run backup |

**If you miss 2+ days of micros in a row:** The goal isn't to "make up" everything. Just do today's. One win breaks the streak.

---

## 💡 The Rules

1. **5 min is the floor.** Not the ceiling — the *minimum*. You can always do more. But 5 min counts as a win.
2. **Move, don't skip.** If the calendar slot passes, reschedule it. A moved session still counts.
3. **No zero days.** Even on your worst day, do one micro. Read one page. Meditate one breath. Type one test.
4. **Consistency > intensity.** "Very few results come from the amount of work in each session. Almost all come from how many sessions you show up."
5. **The bulkier the routine, the slower the results.** Keep it lean. If it feels too heavy, cut something.
