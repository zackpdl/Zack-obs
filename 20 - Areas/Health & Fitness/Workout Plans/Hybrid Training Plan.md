# Hybrid Training Plan

## Goal
Build muscle while improving running performance without trashing recovery.

## Constraints
- Computer Science schedule with long classes
- Gym gets crowded after 4:30 PM
- Thailand heat makes daytime running inefficient
- Current sleep debt is blocking consistency

## Non-Negotiables
1. Sleep comes first.
2. No 5 AM run if sleep is under 6 hours.
3. Do not stack a hard run with a heavy lower-body session.
4. Tired days are for lighter work, not ego work.

## Weekly Structure
- 3 to 4 lifting sessions
- 3 to 4 running sessions
- 1 full rest day

## Recommended Split
### Lifting
- Day 1: Upper
- Day 2: Lower
- Day 3: Upper or Push
- Day 4: Lower or Pull

### Running
- 1 long run
- 1 tempo run
- 1 interval run
- 1 recovery run or walk

## Time Strategy
- Best run slot: after 6:30 PM
- Best lift slot: earlier in the day when possible, before the crowded gym window
- Morning runs only if bedtime is fixed first

## Recovery Targets
- Sleep: 7 to 9 hours
- Protein: 160 g minimum
- Calories: enough to support both lifting and running
- Hard sessions: separated when possible

## Current Priority Order
1. Fix sleep
2. Hit calories and protein
3. Keep weekly consistency
4. Increase training intensity later

## Core Reminder
You do not adapt from suffering through bad recovery. You adapt from repeatable training plus enough sleep and food.
