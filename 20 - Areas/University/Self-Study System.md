## 🎯 The Study Protocol (Per Subject)

### Session Flow

```
STEP 1 — OPEN (2 min)
  Open your study guide in Obsidian
  → Which lesson did you last study? Open that section.

STEP 2 — READ & UNDERSTAND (15-25 min)
  Read the section slowly. Try to explain each concept in your head.
  When you hit a vocabulary word — cover the definition and recall it.

STEP 3 — QUIZ (10 min)
  Ask Hermes: "Quiz me on [topic]"
  → I'll generate 5 questions. Answer them without peeking.
  → I'll tell you what you got right/wrong and explain.

STEP 4 — LOG (2 min)
  Mark your progress in the tracker below.
  → Open **[[20 - Areas/University/Study Progress]]** after each session.

STEP 5 — WHAT'S NEXT? (1 min)
  Log what you'll study next session.
  → "Studied Blockchain Lesson 2. Next: Lesson 3."
```
