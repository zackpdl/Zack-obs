## 🧠 Study Workflow

```
Morning Review ─► Class/Study ─► Note Taking ─► Hermes Quiz ─► NotebookLM
     ↑                                                              │
     └─────────────────────── Weekly Digest ◄───────────────────────┘
```

### 1️⃣ Daily — Morning Prep
Ask Hermes: *"Prepare my study focus for today based on my course schedule"*
→ Hermes checks calendar + surfaces relevant notes

### 2️⃣ In Class — Capture
Take notes directly in course folder → `10 - Projects/Semester 1-2026/{course}/Notes/`

### 3️⃣ After Class — Review & Quiz
Ask Hermes:
- *"Summarize my [course name] notes from today"*
- *"Quiz me on [topic] from my study guide"*
- *"Explain [concept] in simple terms"*

### 4️⃣ Weekly — NotebookLM Export
1. Open study guide in Obsidian
2. Export as PDF → upload to NotebookLM notebook
3. NotebookLM generates Audio Overview (podcast-style)
4. Listen during commute 🚗

### 5️⃣ Exam Prep — AI Study Buddy
- *"Create a cheat sheet for [course] based on my notes"*
- *"Generate 10 practice questions from my study guide"*
- *"Compare [topic A] and [topic B] from my course materials"*
