Total number of states before deciding item i:
    2^i states, because each of the first i items has two choices (pick or skip).

Do earlier choices affect the result of picking item i?
    Yes. The result depends on which previous items were chosen since the capacity changes.

Can this brute-force solution use memoization?
    Yes. Memoization can speed it up by saving results that were already calculated so they are not repeated.
