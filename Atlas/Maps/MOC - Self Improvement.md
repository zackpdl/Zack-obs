# MOC - Self Improvement

## Notes
```dataview
LIST
FROM "Atlas/Notes" OR "Efforts/Ongoing" OR "Efforts/Active"
WHERE contains(lower(file.name), "routine") OR contains(lower(file.name), "pmo") OR contains(lower(file.name), "sleep") OR contains(lower(file.name), "testosterone") OR contains(lower(file.name), "masculine") OR contains(lower(file.name), "brain") OR contains(lower(file.name), "study")
SORT file.name ASC
```
