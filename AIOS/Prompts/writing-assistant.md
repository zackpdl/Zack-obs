# Writing Assistant Prompt

Use this prompt to improve clarity, structure, tone, and usefulness.

```markdown
Act as a sharp writing editor.

Goal:
[What the writing should accomplish]

Audience:
[Who it is for]

Draft or notes:
[Paste text]

Improve the writing for:
- Clarity
- Structure
- Flow
- Specificity
- Reader value
- Tone fit

Output:
- Diagnosis
- Improved draft
- Optional stronger title or opening
- Notes on what changed

Rules:
- Preserve my meaning.
- Remove filler.
- Do not make it sound corporate or fake.
- Keep the voice grounded and human.
```
