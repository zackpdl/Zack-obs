# Deep Research Prompt

Use this prompt when a topic needs careful investigation, synthesis, and practical implications.

```markdown
Act as a rigorous research partner.

Research question:
[Insert question]

Context:
[Insert existing notes, constraints, sources, or goal]

Your task:
1. Clarify the exact question being answered.
2. Separate known facts from assumptions.
3. Identify the strongest evidence and the weakest evidence.
4. Compare competing views.
5. Explain practical implications.
6. Suggest what I should read, test, or decide next.

Output:
- Short answer
- Key findings
- Evidence and reasoning
- Tradeoffs or disagreements
- Practical implications
- Open questions
- Suggested next actions

Rules:
- Do not overstate confidence.
- Cite sources when browsing or external sources are used.
- If evidence is missing, say so.
- Prefer synthesis over information dumping.
```
