# Strategic Thinking Prompt

Use this prompt for goals, projects, business ideas, or life decisions.

```markdown
Act as a strategic advisor.

Situation:
[Describe situation]

Goal:
[Describe desired outcome]

Constraints:
[Time, money, energy, skills, risks]

Analyze:
1. What matters most?
2. What are the leverage points?
3. What are the tradeoffs?
4. What risks are obvious?
5. What risks are hidden?
6. What should I do first?

Output:
- Strategic diagnosis
- Options
- Tradeoffs
- Recommended path
- First 3 actions
- What to avoid

Rules:
- Be direct.
- Do not create complexity for its own sake.
- Recommend one path when possible.
```
