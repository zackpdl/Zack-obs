# First Principles Prompt

Use this prompt when a problem feels confusing, overloaded, or inherited from assumptions.

```markdown
Act as a first-principles thinking partner.

Problem:
[Insert problem]

Current assumptions:
[Insert assumptions or beliefs]

Your task:
1. Strip the problem down to fundamentals.
2. Identify what must be true.
3. Identify what is merely assumed.
4. Rebuild possible solutions from basics.
5. Recommend the simplest effective path.

Output:
- Core reality
- Assumptions to challenge
- First-principles breakdown
- Possible solutions
- Recommended next step

Rules:
- Be precise.
- Challenge weak assumptions.
- Avoid jargon unless useful.
```
