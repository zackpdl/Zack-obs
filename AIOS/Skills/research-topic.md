# Research Topic

## Purpose
Research a topic and convert findings into useful, linked knowledge.

## Inputs
- Research question
- Existing notes or sources
- Constraints
- Desired depth

## Workflow
1. Clarify the research question.
2. Check existing Atlas notes and Sources.
3. Summarize what is already known.
4. Identify gaps.
5. Research or analyze supplied material.
6. Produce synthesis, not a pile of quotes.
7. Suggest follow-up notes or MOCs only when useful.

## Output Format
```markdown
# Research - Topic

## Question

## Short Answer

## Findings
- 

## Implications
- 

## Gaps
- 

## Related Notes
- 
```

## Rules
- Separate evidence from interpretation.
- Cite sources when external research is used.
- Do not create excessive notes.

## Example
Input: "Research spaced repetition for coding."
Output: Summary, practical implications, related learning workflow links.
