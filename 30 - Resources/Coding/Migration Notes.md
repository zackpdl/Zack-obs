# 🗒️ Vault Migration Notes

I have organized your vault to make it more scalable and easier to navigate. Here is a summary of the changes:

## 📂 New Structure
- **`000 Inbox`**: Place for unsorted or temporary files.
- **`010 Personal`**: Routine, wardrobe, and personal identity files.
- **`020 Health`**: Workout plans, meal plans, and the new **Calorie Tracker**.
- **`030 Media`**: Anime lists and other media tracking.
- **`040 University`**: Consolidated all university years and current semester files here.
- **`050 Knowledge`**: (Formerly `000 Zettelkasten`) Your core learning notes.
- **`060 Projects`**: (Formerly `001 Projects`) Active project files.

## 🛠️ New Features
1. **[[Home]]**: A central hub at the root of your vault. It links to your **Brain**, **Bookmarks**, and **Calorie Tracker** for "one-click" access.
2. **[[20 - Areas/Health & Fitness/Nutrition/Calorie Tracker|Calorie Tracker]]**: A dedicated tracker with a 2,500 kcal target goal.
3. **Clean Root**: Moved over 20 files from the root into logical subfolders to reduce clutter.

## 💡 Recommendations
- **Plugins**: To get the most out of the tracker and dashboard, I highly recommend installing the **Dataview** and **Obsidian Columns** (for a prettier dashboard) community plugins.
- **Daily Notes**: I've linked your Daily Notes folder to the dashboard so you can see your recent entries quickly.
