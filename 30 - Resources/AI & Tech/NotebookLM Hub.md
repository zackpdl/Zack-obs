# 📓 NotebookLM Study Hub

> **Google NotebookLM** + **Obsidian** + **Hermes Agent** — your AI-powered study system.

## How It Works

```
Study 📝 → Take notes in Obsidian (PARA structure)
             ↓
        Weekly export → Upload course notes/materials to NotebookLM
             ↓
        NotebookLM generates → Study guides, FAQs, Audio Overviews
             ↓
        Hermes quizzes you → Daily review, spaced repetition
```

## What to Upload to NotebookLM

1. **Lecture notes** (from `10 - Projects/Semester 1-2026/{course}/Notes/`)
2. **Slides & PDFs** (from `10 - Projects/Semester 1-2026/{course}/Materials/`)
3. **Course outlines & syllabi**
4. **Study guides** you've created

## What NotebookLM Gives You

- **Study Guides** → Auto-generated summaries and key concepts
- **FAQ** → Common questions and answers from your materials
- **Audio Overview** → Podcast-style discussion of your notes (great for commutes)
- **Chat** → Ask questions about your specific course materials

## Hermes-Powered Features

- `hermes study quiz` → Generates quiz questions from recent notes
- `hermes study review` → Daily review of what you learned
- `hermes study digest` → Weekly study digest with key concepts
