# Weekly Review

## Purpose
Review the week, maintain the vault, and align active efforts.

## Inputs
- Daily notes from the week
- Inbox
- Active efforts
- Ongoing systems
- Recent Atlas notes

## Workflow
1. Summarize wins and friction.
2. Review active efforts.
3. Decide what continues, pauses, finishes, or archives.
4. Process Inbox.
5. Identify orphan ideas worth linking.
6. Set priorities for next week.

## Output Format
```markdown
## Weekly Review

### Wins
- 

### Lessons
- 

### Efforts
- Continue:
- Pause:
- Finish:

### Inbox Decisions
- 

### Next Week Priorities
1. 
2. 
3. 
```

## Rules
- Do not overload next week.
- Keep priorities few and explicit.
- Link decisions to relevant Efforts.

## Example
Input: Seven daily notes and active project notes.
Output: Weekly review with effort decisions and next week priorities.
